Conditions of Employment:

  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada
  • Must provide and maintain a satisfactory Criminal Record Check, Criminal Record Check with Vulnerable Sector Search, Adult Abuse Registry Check and Child Abuse Registry Check may be required for some positions.
  • Must provide and maintain a satisfactory Enhanced Security Screening may be required for some positions.
  • Completion of satisfactory credit screening and credit check may be required for some positions.

Qualifications:
Essential:

  • Completion of a Business Administration and/or Accounting Diploma or Certificate. A combination of related education, training and experience may be considered
  • Experience performing accounting transactions including accounts payable, accounts receivable, reconciliations, creating financial reports and/or maintaining financial records
  • Strong organizational and time management skills with the ability to prioritize workloads and effectively manage deadlines
  • Ability to work with a high level of accuracy, thoroughness and attention to detail
  • Strong interpersonal skills that facilitate effective working relationships with colleagues, other departments and a variety of stakeholders
  • Ability to manage confidential and sensitive information
  • Proficiency working with Microsoft Office Suite (Outlook, Word, Excel) or similar programs
  • Effective written communication skills with experience drafting and editing correspondence or other communication
  • Effective verbal communication skills to clearly and professionally communicate with internal and external stakeholders
  • Ability to work independently with minimal supervision
  • Experience interpreting and applying policies and/or procedures
Desired:
  • Experience working with SAP (Financial Module) or an equivalent financial management software
  • Ability to read, write and verbally communicate fluently in French

Duties:
Clerk 3 Financial Stream duties will vary depending on the position. Duties may include:

  • Processing financial transactions (Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable) and maintaining accurate financial records;
  • Preparing and processing invoices, receipts, and payments,;
  • Timekeeping entries, payroll related support;
  • acting as a financial clerical liaison/resource for branch/division staff i.e. responding to inquiries and resolving financial issues;
  • Maintaining confidential financial information and records.

Duties will include providing other clerical/administrative support as required.
