Conditions of Employment:

  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada
  • Must provide and maintain a satisfactory Criminal Record Check
  • Must have a valid Manitoba Class 5 Intermediate/Full Driver's licence
  • Must be able to travel occasionally, as required

Qualifications:
Essential:

  • Experience performing a wide variety of administrative and/or clerical functions to support program delivery. 
  • Experience performing accounting transactions including accounts payable, accounts receivable, budget reconciliations and/or creating financial reports.  
  • Experience working as a first point of contact with the public in a customer service-oriented environment and providing service to a diverse clientele.
  • Proficiency working with Microsoft office such as Outlook, Word, Excel or similar software programs.
  • Excellent interpersonal skills to facilitate effective working relationships with a team and the public.
  • Excellent verbal communication skills with the ability to provide information in a clear and concise manner.
  • Excellent written communication skills with the ability to prepare, edit and format correspondence.
  • Ability to work with a high degree of accuracy and strong attention to detail.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills with the ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks with competing deadlines.
  • Ability to manage confidential and sensitive information.
  • Ability to interpret, explain, and apply regulations, policies, and/or procedures.
  • Problem solving skills with the ability to work independently with a high degree of initiative.
Desired:
  • Experience working with SAP or an equivalent financial management software.

Duties:
The Program Support Clerk provides financial, program, and administrative support services to the Central Region. The position plays a pivotal role in ensuring that the office runs in an efficient and effective manner involving a wide range of activities such as financial management, purchasing, third party contract administration by managing vendor information, reconciling payment claim information, unblocking verified payments and assisting with report generation requests; as well as collects client documentation, validates eligibility, and maintains client records.
