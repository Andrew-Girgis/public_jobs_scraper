Conditions of Employment:

  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada
  • Must provide and maintain a satisfactory Criminal Record Check with a Vulnerable Sector Check, Adult Abuse Registry Check and Child Abuse Registry Check.

Qualifications:
Essential:

  • Education or Training in Business administration or an equivalent combination of education, training and experience may be considered.
  • Experience in the supervision of staff, familiarity with human resource management practices and team management skills.  
  • Knowledge of Financial Management including, expenditures, budgeting and cash flow. 
  • Excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to work co-operatively and effectively in a team environment
  • Strong organizational and time management skills, including the ability to independently prioritize workload and work on concurrent assignments with strict deadlines
  • Experience with office management. 
  • Excellent verbal communication skills with the ability to communicate clearly and professionally.
  • Strong written communication skills with the ability to write clear and concise documents with strong attention to detail.
  • Experience using Microsoft Office programs including Word, Excel, Outlook and Adobe.

Desired:
  • Knowledge of government administrative policies, procedures and decision making processes. 
  • Ability to read, write and verbally communicate in French.

Duties:
The Office Manager is responsible for supervising a team of clerical staff in the provision of high quality support services in a public service delivery office. This includes coordinating administrative workload to facilitate the allocation and completion of assignments in an orderly and timely manner and ensuring a positive client service environment.  Reporting to the Director, this position is accountable for a full range of management support including human resources; purchasing, cashflow forecasting and budgeting; preparing authority seeking documents; office equipment and space planning; and records management.
