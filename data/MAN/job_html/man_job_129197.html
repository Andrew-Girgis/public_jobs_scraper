Duties:
Reporting to the Director, Corporate Compliance and Risk Management, the Safety and Risk Officer is responsible for all elements related to the management, facilitation, and administration of the department’s health and safety program. The Safety and Risk Officer ensures compliance with safety and health associated Acts, regulations, provincial and municipal by-laws, and policies to inform and protect the safety and health of workers, contractors, and Manitobans we serve.

The Safety and Risk Officer provides technical expertise, conducts inspections and investigations, right to refuse situations, discriminatory actions, serious incidents and other safety and health related concerns for the department and corporation with a focus on developing and improving legislative, regulatory and policy compliance to eliminate fatalities, injuries and illnesses, and lessen risk to the corporation and department
