The Department of Environment and Climate Change is seeking a customer focused individual to fill the position of Special Programs Coordinator. This is a senior clerical position that works within the Recreation and Education Section to deliver quality public recreation programming to seasonal campers and other groups. Individuals that enjoy working with the public and multi-tasking, often within tight timeframes, are encouraged to apply.  To be considered for this competition you must submit an application form and resume. Conditions of Employment:

  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada
  • Must possess and maintain a valid Class 5 Full Driver's licence.
  • Must be willing and able to travel periodically throughout the province of Manitoba.
  • Must possess and maintain a satisfactory Criminal Record Check
  • Must be willing and able to work extended hours periodically as required.

Qualifications:
Essential:

  • Extensive experience in providing clerical and administrative support for a large-scale public-facing program.
  • Experience providing customer service to diverse segments of the public, including  dealing with complaints and conflict resolution.
  • Proven effective verbal communication skills, including the ability to deal with the public over the phone and in-person
  • Experience with  Microsoft Office Suite including Word, Excel and Outlook or similar software programs.
  • Strong analytical, problem solving and decision-making skills.
  • Experience preparing financial reports and/or reconciliations.
  • Ability to work with high level of accuracy, thoroughness, and attention to detail.
  • Effective organizational skills with the ability to multitask while working on concurrent assignments with strict deadlines.
  • Strong interpersonal skills, including the ability to work cooperatively in a team environment and with the public.
  • Proven effective written communication skills
  • Experience interpreting and applying policies and procedures.
  • Ability to work independently with minimal supervision.

Desired:
  • Knowledge of seasonal camping and Manitoba's provincial park campgrounds.
  • Ability to read, write, and verbally communicate fluently in French

Duties:
The Special Programs Coordinator position coordinates and administers approximately 1500 campsites in the annual Seasonal Camping Program and related public draws, as well as the Permanent Trailer Village Program and manages the flow of program information to customers, field office, branch and public information officers. It is responsible for the reconciliation of draw records and $1.5 million in revenues to the department. It will also co-ordinate the Commercial Concessionaire Program and the Campground Host Program with field staff and the public. Responsibilities also include the preparation and development of annual parks statistics. Lastly, it will support the Parks Reservation Service and the nightly camping program during peak times.
